Pocket Nurse Wiki

Description:
This mod adds a new post-Mech accessory that allows you to heal using hotkey (default is 'h', but you can change it)
for price of 2 silver per hp. It also gives some buffs for short amount of time
Content:

1. Items


How to change hotkey:
1. Inside of the world
- press escape (to open your inventory)
- click on "Settings"
- open Mod Configuration
- find "Pocket Nurse: Hotkey Config" and click it
- here you can edit it
2. Main menu
- click "Workshop"
- click "Manage Mods"
- find "Pocket Nurse"
- click gear icon
- and here you can edit it (note that you can use slider or press '>' next to "Notification" to edit it with text field)
